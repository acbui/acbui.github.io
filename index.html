DRUNKWALK is a first-person game in which the player must race to the bed before passing out on the floor. The player must use the Sony Move controller (strapped to their chest) to tilt and rotate to move throughout the levels. DrunkWalk can be played in single-player modes (using the Move or the Mouse) or in split-screen multiplayer (Move controllers only - race or party mode).
This game was developed in 6 weeks in Unity, using C#, with Winnie Song.

LONG-DISTANCE R’LYEHTIONSHIP is a Lovecraft-themed dating sim set in space where the player must either obey the captain’s orders to continue their journey to the core of a galactic anomaly or sabotage it through the tasks given by the mysterious creature threatened by their mission.
Developed in Unity (C#), with Ashwath George, Andrew Goudreau, Kim Hoang, and Tuuli Saarinen.

SPACEBRO JUSTICE ROCKET is a two-player cooperative rhythm game using a set of custom-made glove controllers where the players must high-five, pound fists, and bump elbows to defeat a robot overlord. Spacebro Justice Rocket was chosen to be further developed by Nick Kornek and Louis Sciannamblo in phase two of the Critical Hit Incubator.
Developed in Unity (C#) and an Arduino board, with Nick Kornek, Josh Raab, and Louis Sciannamblo.

WEIRD NEIGHBOURHOOD is a point-and-click 2D exploration game where the player interacts with different parts and characters of the neighbourhood to discover different hand-drawn animations and multi-layered soundscapes that liven up seemingly abandoned houses.
Developed in Unity, using C#, with Pierre Depaz, Hamish Lambert, and Ben Swinden.

HERE KITTY KITTY! is a runner where the player must use a mouse to guide a cat through Montreal back to her owner, while avoiding inconvenient and harmful obstacles. Luckily, Kitty has 9 lives and can collect fishes for a bonus power. Bring her home fast for the highest score!
Developed in 6 weeks using GameMaker (scripting in the engine’s own language GML), with assets made in PyxelEdit, for the Pixelles Incubator, helping women make their first game on their own.
